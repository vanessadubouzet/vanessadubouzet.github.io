YourDrobe

Your personal closet companion mobile app.

Some closets are very very messy! As well, some “Fashionistas” might run the risk of buying the same item twice because they forgot they already owned it! Our task is to develop an application that can not only catalog your library, but prepare outfits in advance, take a picture of an item of clothing and see similar items, and add friends and see their own wardrobe.

Goals for the project

  • Provide an Easy Friendly to use application that can help you organize your closet
  • Be able to search through your library of clothes as well as take a picture of your clothes and see similar results
  • A system that helps you choose your outfits based on the clothes you have in your clothing library
  • A system that lets you make friends and see their clothing library as well as make outfits for them based on the clothes that they have.
  • Managing the clothes easily
  • Possibly making more available space inside the close

Wireframe | Prototype

Design Rationale

Logo

  • Hanger with the name of the application
  • Conveys the purpose of the app clearly

Setting

  • Settings can be accessed from most screens
  • Allows users to customize their app without unnecessary navigation

Pictures

  • Grid-style pictures encourage users to focus on each picture
  • Allows users to recognize which clothes are stored in this app

Navigation Bar

  • Standard design for mobile apps and provide a familiar interface to most users
  • It allows users to access the main features easily and from any page
  • The obvious signifier allows users to navigate these features without any training

Closet and Outfit Menus

  • Enables users to access both closets and outfits via one menu, reducing time and effort
  • Design allows users to access each category without opening many menus

User Persona / Scenario

Helen has been collecting clothes for over 25 years! Currently she has everything she has ever worn recorded into a binder that has over 500 pages She thinks to herself that there has to be a better way. Unfortunately due to her limited technological experience, she has a friend search on her phone and install an app titled Yourdrobe that promises an easy way to track clothing as well as create outfits that you can simulate! Thanks to this, Helen now has her entire wardrobe available at her fingertips. Which can be very helpful when she is out shopping and tries to buy an item that she already has.
