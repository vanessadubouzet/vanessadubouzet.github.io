Problem Statement

In stock market trading, human traders often rely on manual analysis and decision-making, which can be prone to biases, inefficiencies, and slow reactions to market fluctuations. The large volume of data available can overwhelm traders, making it difficult to extract actionable insights in a timely manner. Moreover, inaccurate predictions can lead to poor investment decisions and financial losses.

Proposed Solution

The ATS offers an automated trading solution that collects real-time data from multiple financial APIs and stores it in a structured data warehouse. This data is used to train machine learning models, which can generate accurate stock market predictions. By automating data analysis and prediction, ATS aims to provide traders with better insights and reduce manual intervention in decision-making, ultimately leading to improved trading outcomes.
