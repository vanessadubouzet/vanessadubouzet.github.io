Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle with managing their wardrobes effectively, leading to time wasted deciding what to wear, clutter, and underutilized clothing items. This can be particularly frustrating for people with busy lifestyles, resulting in the repeated use of a limited selection of outfits despite owning numerous clothing pieces. The lack of a centralized system for cataloging clothing can also make it difficult to visualize new outfit combinations and maximize the wardrobe's potential.

Proposed Solution

YourDrobe provides a comprehensive platform that allows users to digitally store and organize their clothing items. Users can upload images of their clothes, categorize them, and create outfit combinations for different occasions. With features such as outfit scheduling and a search function, the platform enables users to plan their wardrobe efficiently. This solution not only helps in reducing decision fatigue but also promotes better use of existing clothing items, making getting dressed each day more fun and less time-consuming.
