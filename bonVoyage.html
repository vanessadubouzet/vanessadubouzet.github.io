Problem Statement

Spontaneous trips, like a getaway to Las Vegas, can become overwhelming when trying to decide on the best places to visit. Travelers often struggle to find good restaurants, popular photo spots, or well-organized itineraries. Searching for reviews on each place individually can be tedious, with no centralized platform to provide quick, real-time recommendations based on actual user experiences and travel tips.

Proposed Solution

BonVoyage simplifies the travel experience by consolidating key travel information into one easy-to-use platform. Users can search for a city, explore recommended places, and organize their trips all in one app. In addition, users can share and discover travel tips for each city, including advice on the best hotels, local apps, transportation options, and hidden gems. The most helpful tips, determined by upvotes, will appear at the top, ensuring that travelers always see the best suggestions first. This system is modeled after Reddit, but focuses exclusively on travel advice and experiences.
